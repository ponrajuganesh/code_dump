About Us

Handycart is your one-stop shop for products needed on a regular basis. Need milk on Tuesday? We got! Need milk and yogurt delivered every friday on a biweekly basis? We got that covered as well.

Handycart was founded in 2010 by 5 Texas A&M University graduates. What started as a summer-project turned into a full-fledged company boasting of a wide array of products, merchants and customers every year.

How it works

Step 1:

Choose from an assortment of goods ranging from chocolates to breads.

Step 2:

Schedule your delivery - the day and frequency. Your subscription is saved!

Step 3:

Your products are delivered. See easy as 1,2,3!
